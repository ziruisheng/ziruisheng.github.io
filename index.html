Research Interests

Areas of Focus

01

Tensor Networks & Quantum Algorithms

Development and application of tensor network methods and quantum computing algorithms for many-body quantum systems.

02

Open Quantum System Dynamics

Investigating transport processes under quantum many-body interactions, with emphasis on charge and energy transfer in complex molecular systems.

03

2D Materials First-Principles Calculations

First-principles calculations of two-dimensional materials, exploring their electronic, optical, and transport properties.
